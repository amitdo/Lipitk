Lipitk by hplabsindia

Lipi Toolkit (LipiTk) is an open source toolkit for online Handwriting Recognition (HWR), created and maintained by HP Labs India. The word 'lipi' is Sanskrit for script. The primary objective of LipiTk is to simplify the creation and deployment of HWR technology for new scripts. Other broad goals include supporting collaborative HWR R&D in academic and industrial settings, acting as a tool for user interface research, supporting commercial HWR development, promotion of standard ink representations and interfaces, promotion of sharing & reuse of tools, algorithms, code and handwriting datasets, and promotion of product and solution development. Lipi Toolkit was first released internally in Oct 2005 and on SourceForge in Apr 2006 and Nov 2011 on Github. Over the years we have added tools such as Lipi IDE to simplify creation of recognition engines for non-specialists, and prebuilt recognizers for different scripts that application developers can download and use in their applications. Various components of Lipi Toolkit are being used around the world today.
